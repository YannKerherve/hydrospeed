Our Project

This year, our project is to win the international Hydrocontest competition with our very low-consumption, eco-designed boat.

LinkedIn Instagram

A technological challenge

Our 2.5-meter prototype integrates sustainable propulsion solutions and a design optimized for performance. Every element is conceived for the future of the maritime industry.

The competition

Hydrocontest By ENSM is an international student competition for the creation of prototypes of environmentally friendly, high-performance ships. These ships compete against each other in various courses (energy-saving race, maneuverability, speed with limited energy).

Our solutions

Our hull is particularly adapted to minimize the consumption of the electric propulsion motor. With its "Wave-Piercing" shape, the bow wave should be limited as much as possible.
