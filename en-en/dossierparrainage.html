Who are we?

Hydrospeed Team is a French association (law 1901) of students from ENSM Marseille. It brings together 7 students and 2 professors around the Hydrocontest 2026 project. Together, we innovate for a sustainable maritime future.

Our prototype

Designed with flax fiber in a shipyard in Martigues, our boat uses a "wave-piercing" hull with an activatable bulb. It embodies performance, durability, and respect for the environment.

ENSM

Founded in 1571, ENSM trains merchant navy officers. Recognized worldwide, it awards engineering degrees and offers cutting-edge training (ice, gas, propulsion...). We are all students at the Marseille site to become Multipurpose Officers.

  • Contribute to the decarbonization of the sector
  • Promote applied research
